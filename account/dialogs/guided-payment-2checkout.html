To Configure your 2Checkout account..

  • Login to your 2Checkout account
  • Head to the account tab and then click site management
  • Ensure you have selected 'Off' for demo settings
  • Choose the 'Direct Return (Our URL)' option under Direct Return
  • Enter the below URL in Approved URL field http://dev.cloudcharge.com/services/ duosoftware.ipn.service/2checkout_ipn.php
  • Click on Save Changes and you are done
